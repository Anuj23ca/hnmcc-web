About Our Club

Our Story

Hamro Nepali McCowan Cricket Club (HN-MCC) is a Scarborough-based cricket team proudly founded by Nepali students who share a deep passion for the sport. What started as casual matches during the summer of 2024 quickly grew into something bigger ‚Äî and in 2025, we officially established HN-MCC as a cricket club.

Today, we are a growing community of over 100 members united by our love for cricket, cultural connection, and team spirit. As proud representatives of the Nepali youth in Canada, we aim to create opportunities for friendly competition, personal growth, and community engagement both on and off the field.
